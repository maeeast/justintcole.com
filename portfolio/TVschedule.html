Proposed Schedule for SciFi Channel - 1 Year Cycle
Below is a television programming schedule for the SciFi Channel (circa 2007)
This was drafted when the SciFi Channel abandoned their core audience to seek a more lucrative programming model. The schedule below arose due to an online discussion where I was told that television programming choices are more difficult than they seem and I would not be able to do better than the choices the channel made. I assembled the below schedule in approximately one hour.
If I had information on how much each of the syndicated shows listed, and the original content, cost to product and air I could have included that information. Additionally, I could, easily, have included an analysis of those costs in comparison to the expected advertising revenue for the shows during the listed time slots if that information was readily available.

Please note that this may have been edited from its original content to protect the confidential information of the applicable employer.
