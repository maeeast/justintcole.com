Mathematical Business Model
Below is a Mathematical Business Model created to generate predictions of subscriber-base, revenue, and profit for a service that required a physical product to use it. (circa 2007)
Initially, this was three static charts similar to those found on the "Summary" and "Valuation" worksheets. I realized, as I was asked to update them often for differing scenarios, that I should build a means to do so quickly and easily. As a result, I made the initial charts dynamic so that the mathematics calculated based on the simple inputs.
My employer expected a several-hour turnaround on each new simulation request. Automating the simple charts allowed me add new features with each pass. Eventually, I had a much more powerful tool and I was able to offer more insight, regarding changing scenarios, with a near-instantaneous turn-around time.
